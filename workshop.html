WORKSHOP

I hosted a workshop “Protest Mooncakes”, heavily inspired by Hong Kong’s Anti-Extradition Mooncakes, and my Singaporean-Malaysian-Chinese heritage.

Participants create and consume personalized Protest Mooncakes with their own political messages using 3D-printed letters, numbers, symbols and designs.

This workshop encourages participants to merge personal, cultural, and political expression through food.
